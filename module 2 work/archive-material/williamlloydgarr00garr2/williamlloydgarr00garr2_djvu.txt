TO THE PUBLIC.

There are numerous difficulties always occurring with men 
of business, as also with the mechanic, wherever they trust 
apparently small and unimportant items to their memory alone, 
as they are often obliged to do, when transacting business at a 
distance, and when their regular accounts are not near at hand. 
Being aware of this, the Publishers were induced to present to 
the Public something that would serve to obviate the difficulties, 
and they trust that the following work will not be unacceptable 
to them in their business transactions. 

The Astronomical Calculations, by J. L. Downes, are cal- 
culated for Boston and New York, will answer for any part 
of New England, and will be explained by referring to the top 
of each page. 

On the last page will be found a Table of Valuation of all 
those Coins which were made Legal Tender by Act of Con- 
gress of 1834, both according to the New and the Old Standard. 
This Table will be of great service to persons in all kinds of 
business, and can be depended upon as being correct. 



The letters M. m. m. denote Morning — A. a. a. Afternoon.
