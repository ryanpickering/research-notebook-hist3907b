| = "or"
\b = beginning of word/end of word for textwrangler
parentheses = "or" within a word or of a phrase, ie. gr(a|e)y or (that dog)|(that cat)
. = any character
+ = find any number of previous character... ie. do+g = dog, doog, dooog, etc. (do)+g = dog, dodog, dododog, etc. 

groups: each bracketed thing becomes a numbered group. 

for more... http://regexlib.com/CheatSheet.aspx
